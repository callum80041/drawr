# playdrawr — FAQ

For press, website, and customer support use.

---

## The Basics

**What is playdrawr?**
playdrawr is a free web platform for running a World Cup 2026 sweepstake. It handles the draw, the leaderboard, and the scoring automatically — so the person organising it doesn't have to manage a spreadsheet throughout the tournament.

**Is it really free?**
Yes. No paid plans, no entry fees, no percentage of the prize pot. Free.

**Do I need to download an app?**
No. playdrawr is a website. It works in any browser on any device.

**Do participants need to create an account?**
No. Participants access everything via a shared link. Only the organiser needs an account.

---

## Setting Up

**How long does it take to set up?**
Under 60 seconds for a basic sweepstake. Add a name, set an optional entry fee, and you're done. Adding participants takes longer depending on how many you have.

**How many participants can I have?**
Up to 48 on the free plan — one per World Cup team, which is the maximum for a full sweepstake.

**Can I run a sweepstake with fewer than 48 participants?**
Yes. If you have fewer participants than teams, some participants get assigned more than one team. Points from all their teams count.

**Can I add email addresses for participants?**
Yes, but it's optional. If you add an email address, that participant gets their team emailed to them automatically when the draw completes.

---

## The Draw

**How does the draw work?**
Teams are assigned randomly using a Fisher-Yates shuffle — the fairest possible method. Each team is equally likely to go to any participant. There is no weighting, no bias.

**Can I watch the draw live?**
Yes. The draw runs as a live animation — each team is revealed one by one with the country flag, team name, and the participant's name. You can run it on a screen with your group watching.

**Can I choose teams manually instead of randomly?**
Yes. There's a manual drag-and-drop mode where you assign teams to participants yourself.

**What if someone joins after the draw has run?**
The organiser has three options: manually reassign teams, automatically rebalance (takes one team from anyone who has more than one), or run a full redraw with everyone included.

**Can I redo the draw?**
Yes, at any point before the tournament starts.

## Scoring

**How does scoring work?**
Your team earns points for every match they win. Points increase as the tournament progresses:

| Round | Points per win |
|-------|---------------|
| Group stage | 3 pts |
| Round of 32 | 5 pts |
| Round of 16 | 8 pts |
| Quarter-final | 12 pts |
| Semi-final | 15 pts |
| Win the World Cup | 25 pts |

Draws earn 1 point in the group stage. Losses earn nothing.

**Does the World Cup winner always win the sweepstake?**
Almost certainly yes. With 25 points just for lifting the trophy, plus points accumulated through six knockout wins, the person who drew the champion almost always finishes first.

**How often does the leaderboard update?**
Match results are synced once daily. During the tournament, this means standings update overnight after each matchday.

**Who updates the scores?**
We do, automatically. Scores come from a live football data provider and update without any input from the organiser.

## Money and Payments

**Can I run a money pot sweepstake?**
Yes. Set an entry fee when creating the sweepstake. playdrawr tracks who has paid and shows the total prize pot.

**Does playdrawr handle the money?**
No. The money stays between you and your participants — collect it however you normally would (cash, bank transfer, whatever works for your group). We just track who's paid.

**Is this gambling?**
No. A sweepstake is a social competition. playdrawr is not a bookmaker and is not regulated as one.

## Privacy and Data

**What data do you collect?**
For organisers: name and email address. For participants: name and, optionally, email address. We don't collect payment information.

**Do you share data with third parties?**
We use Supabase (database), Vercel (hosting), and Resend (email delivery). We do not sell or share data with anyone else.

**Will participants get marketing emails?**
No. The only emails participants receive are: their team assignment after the draw, and any leaderboard update notifications the organiser triggers. No newsletters, no marketing.

**Can I delete my data?**
Yes. Email headcoach@playdrawr.co.uk and we'll delete your account and all associated data.

## Technical

**What browsers does it support?**
Any modern browser — Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge. Works on mobile and tablet.

**Does it work on mobile?**
Yes. The participant view is fully optimised for mobile.

**What tournament data does it cover?**
All 48 teams and 104 matches of FIFA World Cup 2026 (USA, Canada, Mexico). Group stage, round of 32, round of 16, quarter-finals, semi-finals, third-place play-off, and final.
