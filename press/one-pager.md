## What it is

playdrawr is a free web platform for running a World Cup 2026 sweepstake. It replaces the spreadsheet, the hat, and the WhatsApp thread with an animated draw, a live leaderboard, and automatic scoring.

## The problem

Every four years, someone has to organise the sweepstake. That means:
- Collecting names over WhatsApp
- Drawing teams from a hat (or a spreadsheet with a random function nobody trusts)
- Manually updating scores throughout a six-week tournament
- Answering "who did I get?" repeatedly for the entire month of June

## The solution

playdrawr handles all of it:

1. **Organiser creates a sweepstake** — takes under 60 seconds
2. **Adds participants** — name and optional email address
3. **Runs the draw** — live animated reveal, one team at a time, in front of the group
4. **Shares a link** — the leaderboard updates automatically as matches are played

## Key numbers

- **48** nations covered (full World Cup 2026 draw)
- **104** matches tracked
- **30 seconds** average setup time
- **£0** cost — free, no paid plans
- **0** logins required for participants

## What makes it different

**The draw is an event.** Other tools just assign teams. playdrawr reveals them one by one with a live animation — flags, team names, participant names — designed to be run on a screen with the whole group watching.

**Zero admin after draw day.** Scoring is automatic. The leaderboard updates itself. The organiser's job ends when they hit the draw button.

**Participants need nothing.** No app, no account, no friction. One link shared on WhatsApp covers everything.

## Who it's for

Office sweepstake organisers, pub groups, families, sports clubs — anyone running a World Cup sweepstake for 2 to 48 people.

## Business model

Currently free to grow the user base ahead of World Cup 2026. AdSense integration planned. Future premium features (higher participant limits, multi-tournament support) may be introduced post-tournament.
