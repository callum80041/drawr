## playdrawr Launches Free World Cup 2026 Sweepstake Platform Ahead of Tournament Kick-off

*British-built platform lets anyone run a fully automated football sweepstake in under a minute — with live scoring, animated draws, and a shareable leaderboard that updates in real time*

playdrawr (playdrawr.co.uk) today launched a free web platform that takes the admin out of running a World Cup sweepstake. With the FIFA World Cup 2026 beginning in June, the platform lets organisers set up a sweepstake, invite participants, and run a live animated draw — all without any technical knowledge, and entirely for free.

### How it works

An organiser visits playdrawr.co.uk, creates a sweepstake in under a minute, and adds their participants with optional email addresses. When everyone's in, they hit the draw button: each of the 48 World Cup teams is assigned to a participant in a live, animated reveal — one by one, with flags, team names, and a countdown — mirroring the drama of the real draw.

From that moment, the platform takes over. As matches are played, points are automatically calculated and the leaderboard updates. Participants follow along via a shareable link — no account, no app, no faff.

### Key features

- **Animated draw** — teams revealed one by one in a live sequence, with speed control and skip-to-end option
- **Live leaderboard** — updates automatically as real match results come in
- **Group standings** — full group stage tables with qualification tracking
- **Fixture tracker** — every match, score, and kickoff time in one place
- **Email notifications** — participants receive their team via email the moment the draw completes
- **Payment tracking** — organisers can mark who's paid their entry fee and track the prize pot
- **Multiple draw modes** — fully random, or manually assign teams via drag-and-drop
- **No login for participants** — share one link, everyone can follow along
- **Completely free** — no paid tiers, no catch

### Points system

Teams earn points for every match won, increasing as the tournament progresses:

| Round | Points |
|-------|--------|
| Group stage win | 3 pts |
| Round of 32 win | 5 pts |
| Round of 16 win | 8 pts |
| Quarter-final win | 12 pts |
| Semi-final win | 15 pts |
| Win the World Cup | 25 pts |

The platform covers all 48 nations and 104 matches across the 2026 tournament, which spans the United States, Canada, and Mexico.

### Background

playdrawr was built to solve a problem its founder had every four years — the sweepstake spreadsheet that nobody updates, the WhatsApp thread that spirals, and the draw that happens via folded bits of paper in a cup. The platform is designed to feel effortless for organisers and genuinely fun for participants, with a tone that doesn't take football — or itself — too seriously.
