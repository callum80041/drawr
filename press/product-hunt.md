# playdrawr — Product Hunt Launch Copy

## Tagline
Run your World Cup 2026 sweepstake in 30 seconds. Free.

## Description

Every World Cup, someone has to organise the sweepstake. That person used to need a spreadsheet, a hat, and infinite patience for WhatsApp messages asking "who did I get again?"

playdrawr fixes this.

🎲 **Create** a sweepstake in under a minute
👥 **Add** your participants (up to 48 — one per team)
▶️ **Run the draw** — animated, one team at a time, with flags and everything
📊 **Share a link** — live leaderboard, fixtures, and group standings, no login needed
⚽ **Scoring updates automatically** as real matches are played

Built for offices, pub groups, families, and anyone who's ever lost the sweepstake spreadsheet.

**Completely free. No paid plans. Just football.**
